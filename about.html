Scrum Poker

About

I made this tool because I did not find any scrum poker tools that can be shared between team members that have different operating systems on their devices and that do not have any advertisement in it.

Try it out

You can use this tool right in your browser:

And if you prefer other values for your estimations, you can specify them in "cards" parameter separated by semicolon:

  • https://800147.github.io/scrum-poker/?cards=1;2;3;5;8;13;21
  • https://800147.github.io/scrum-poker/?cards=🪨;📃;✂️;🦎;🖖
